Welcome to the Gap Analysis
This tool forms part of the Business Process Reengineering (BPR) engagement currently underway at KZNGFA. Your input is critical to identifying process gaps and shaping the future-state design.

What is a process gap?
A gap is a point in your current (as-is) process where something is missing, unclear, duplicated, inefficient, or not aligned to policy or best practice.

What you will be asked to do

  • Select the department you represent
  • Work through each of your department's documented processes
  • Review the process map and identify gaps by category
  • Click the step in the process map where the gap occurs
  • Describe the gap and suggest a mitigation where possible

Four gap categories

⚡ Service Delivery
Gaps in how services reach stakeholders
⚙️ Automation / Tools
Manual steps that could be digitised
📈 Productivity
Inefficiencies, bottlenecks, duplication
🏛️ Governance
Control, policy, and oversight gaps

Note: Your responses are saved locally in your browser as you go. You can return and continue where you left off, provided you use the same browser and device.
