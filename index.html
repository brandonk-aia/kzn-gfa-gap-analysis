Welcome to the Gap Analysis
This survey forms part of the Business Process Reengineering (BPR) engagement currently underway at KZNGFA. Your input is critical to identifying process gaps and shaping the future-state design.

What is a process gap?
A gap is a point in your current (as-is) process where something is missing, unclear, duplicated, inefficient, or not aligned to policy or best practice. Gaps may relate to systems, people, controls, documentation, or workflows.

What you will be asked to do

  • Select the department you represent
  • Work through each of your department's documented processes
  • Indicate whether gaps exist and describe them briefly
  • Suggest mitigations or solutions where possible

Note: Your responses are saved locally in your browser as you go. You can return to this page and continue where you left off, provided you use the same browser and device.

This survey covers the processes documented during the as-is mapping phase. If a process is not listed, it may be captured in a separate submission or follow-up session. For queries contact your BPR project coordinator.
